560. Subarray Sum Equals K

presum + hashmap 的方法

sum[i, j] = sum[0, j] - sum[0, i - 1] --> sum[0, i - 1] = sum[0, j] - sum[i, j]

k sum hashmap的key --> hashmap的key = sum - k
